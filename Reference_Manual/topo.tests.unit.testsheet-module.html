topo.tests.unit.testsheet¶

Module¶

Unit tests for sheet and sheetcoords.

class topo.tests.unit.testsheet.ExtraSheetTests(methodName='runTest')[source]¶

Bases: unittest.case.TestCase

sheet tests that were written independently of the framework above.

Each of these tests runs once and is independent of the rest of the file.

test_coordinate_position()[source]¶

these tests duplicate some of the earlier ones, except these use a matrix with non-integer (right-left) and (top-bottom). This is an important test case for the definition of density; without it, the tests above could be passed by a variety of sheet2matrix, bounds2shape functions, etc.

CEBALERT: transfer the box to TestBox3Coordinates and have these tests run in the framework.

test_matrix2sheet_center()[source]¶

Check that the center in Sheet coordinates corresponds to the center in continuous matrix coordinates.

test_matrix2sheet_left_top()[source]¶

Check that Sheet’s (0,0) is the top-left of the matrix.

Check that just outside the top-left in matrix coordinates comes back to Sheet coordinates that are outside the BoundingBox.

test_matrix2sheet_right_bottom()[source]¶

Check that Sheet’s (right,bottom) is the bottom-right in matrix coordinates i.e. [rbound,cbound]

Check that just outside the bottom-right in matrix coordinates comes back to Sheet coordinates that are outside the BoundingBox.

test_matrix2sheet_sheet2matrix()[source]¶

Check that sheet2matrix() is the inverse of matrix2sheet().

test_matrixidx2sheet_center()[source]¶

The row and col index of the center unit in the matrix should come back as the Sheet coordinates of the center of that center unit.

test_matrixidx2sheet_left_bottom()[source]¶

The bottom-left matrix cell [0,rbound] should be given back in Sheet coordinates at the center of that cell.

The cell [last_row+1,-1] outside this corner should come back out of the BoundingBox.

test_matrixidx2sheet_left_top()[source]¶

The top-left matrix cell [0,0] should be given back in Sheet coordinates at the center of that cell.

The cell [-1,-1] outside this corner should come back out of the BoundingBox

test_matrixidx2sheet_right_bottom()[source]¶

The bottom-right matrix cell [cbound,rbound] should be given back in Sheet coordinates at the center of that cell.

The cell [last_row+1,last_col+1] outside this corner should come back out of the BoundingBox.

test_matrixidx2sheet_right_top()[source]¶

The top-right matrix cell [cbound,0] should be given back in Sheet coordinates at the center of that cell.

The cell [-1,last_col+1] outside this corner should come back out of the BoundingBox.

test_matrixidx2sheet_sheet2matrixidx()[source]¶
